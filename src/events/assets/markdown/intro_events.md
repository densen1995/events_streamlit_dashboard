Welcome to  **Stockholm Puls** ,your guide to discovering live experiences across Sweden's capital city.

This dataset captures events sourced from **Ticketmaster**, **Visit Stockholm**, **Fasching**, and **Berns**, spanning performances, exhibitions, concerts, sporting events, and more from 2026 through 2027.

- **Segments:** Arts & Theatre, Music, Nightlife, Sports, and Miscellaneous
- **Venues:** From iconic Stockholm stages like Cirkus and Berns to intimate neighbourhood spaces
- **Booking Status:** Real-time status tracking , on sale, off sale, rescheduled, or cancelled
- **Geographic Data:** Every event is mapped to a precise latitude and longitude for venue discovery

Whether you are planning your next night out, researching the Stockholm events scene, or building a recommendation engine, this dashboard gives you the tools to explore, filter, and discover.

*Data sourced from Ticketmaster, Visit Stockholm, Fasching, and Berns public listings.*
